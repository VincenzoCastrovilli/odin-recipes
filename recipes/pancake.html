Pancake

Description

Delicious pancakes to start the day in a sweet day

Easy and fast to prepare

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 250ml
  • Flour - 200g
  • Sugar - 1 tb.
  • Baking powder - 1 tb.

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
